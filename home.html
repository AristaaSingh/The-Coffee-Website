A Tale of Aroma: Coffee



Coffee is a popular beverage made from roasted coffee beans, known for its dark color, bitterness, and slight acidity, attributed to its caffeine content. It holds the highest sales among hot drinks globally. The process involves separating the seeds of the Coffea plant's fruits to produce green coffee beans, which are then roasted, ground, and steeped in hot water.
